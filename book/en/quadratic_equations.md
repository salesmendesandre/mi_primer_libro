# Quadratic Equations

Quadratic equations have the general form:

$$
ax^2 + bx + c = 0
$$ (eq-quadratic)

Where $a$, $b$, and $c$ are constants and $a \neq 0$.

## General formula

The solution to equation {eq}`eq-quadratic` is given by the general formula:

$$
x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}
$$
